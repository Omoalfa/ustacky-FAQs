FAQs

At Ustacky, we aim to kick start and enhance careers through digital learning. Our microdegree programs feature a wide range of essential courses plus additional resources to give you an edge.

Our courses are taught by an expert-led team of instructors. And our hands are always on deck to ensure industry-relevant curriculums are available for you in the shortest timeframe possible.Ensure you check back regularly for the latest microdegree programs we have in stock.

Some of your most frequently asked questions are answered below:

How do I enroll for a Ustacky course / microdegree program?

Ustacky courses / microdegree programs come in two broad categories; Limited Access and Unlimited Access. With Limited Access, you begin and end a program/course within a defined period.

With Unlimited Access, you learn at your own pace. You can start the program/course whenever you like, and there are no completion deadlines.

Our courses can be accessed from any internet-enabled device or platform including desktops, laptops, and tablets.

Once you enroll in a course, you will receive a confirmation email with links to log into your Ustacky account. From there, you can navigate to your Courses/Programs page amongst others.

To log into your Ustacky account, please click here.

What do Ustacky programs/courses include?

All Ustacky programs/courses are taught by expert instructors. Courses are mostly videos but can also include, slides, text and other resources as required. Additionally, we can include practice tests and coding exercises when necessary. All courses have a discussion dashboard through which learners can interact with each other and also post questions to the instructor.

Is Ustacky an accredited institution?

Ustacky is not yet an accredited institution but we offer courses taught by certified software development professionals. All courses include a certificate of completion acknowledged by A-list partners. Certificates can be saved as a .pdf or .jpg file so that you can easily share your accomplishment.

Does Ustacky organize bootcamps?

We do not organize bootcamps at the moment but we are working on organizing a few in the nearest future. Information on that will be available on all our channels once we have fixed a date.

Can I get a job at Ustacky?

We are not recruiting at the moment. However, vacancies are regularly posted on our ‘Jobs’ Page and are shared across our channels when available.
